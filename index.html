About

Building AI
that matters.

I’m an AI & Data consultant at EY Melbourne, working at the intersection of AI, data and enterprise delivery — and a 2025 AI Rising Star (Australia) award nominee.

I came in from an engineering PhD — four years of mixed-methods research and predictive modelling, where I learned to translate something complex into something a non-technical audience can act on. That translation muscle is what I use most days now: taking enterprise AI from an ambiguous problem to a delivered, in-production product.

At EY I design and deliver AI and data products for large enterprise clients — scoping initiatives, shaping solutions, and getting them into production. I’m currently building depth in generative AI, agentic AI, Microsoft Copilot and next-generation business application solutions.
